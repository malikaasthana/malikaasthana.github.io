Malika Asthana

Policy, Communications, and Events

Malika Asthana was born and raised in Markham, a suburb outside of the city of Toronto in Canada. She holds an Honours Bachelor of Social Sciences with specialisation in International Studies and Modern Languages from the University of Ottawa, and is proficient in French and Spanish. She is currently based in London, pursuing a Master of Science in International Relations at the London School of Economics and Political Science. An aspiring policy wonk, she has an interest in critical security studies, foreign policy analysis, and public policy development. Learn more about her experiences in academia, government, and non-government organisations below.
