Amanda Marochko

Creative technology & events

Born and raised in Ottawa, Canada, I currently live and breathe in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, working in the marketing department of a major association within the international meetings industry. With a B. Soc. Sci in Political Science and Communications and a post-graduate diploma in Interactive Multimedia, I enjoy being able to pair theoretical knowledge with hands on application. This has led me to become proficient in a multitude of computer languages, graphic design, videography and application development. I am currently enrolled in a Master of Science programme in International Event Management and I am also in the process of completing my Digital Event Strategist (DES) Certification.
